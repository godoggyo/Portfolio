Hello and Welcome to my website!

My name is Jacob Nachman and I am a Computer Engineering student at Iowa State University. While I am a computer engineering student, I also have a Sales Engineering minor and have a strong passion for computer programming and software development. From there I did my research and decided to take a Java class in high school to see if I would be interested in programming. I absolutely loved it and took a class at a local community college about networking, programming, and computer hardware. In the fall of 2015 I enrolled in my freshman year at Iowa State University. When I went to ISU I decided to study computer engineering. In my career here at Iowa State, I have done various projects, both inside and outside of school, ranging from things like embedded programming to things like this website. I really have a passion for hardware and software development and hopefully I can show you some of this passion through this website.
